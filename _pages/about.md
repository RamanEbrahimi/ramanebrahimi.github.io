I am a PhD student at the [University of California, San Diego](https://ucsd.edu/) in Electrical and Computer Engineering department supervised by Prof. [Parinaz Naghizadeh](https://parinazn.com/). Previously, I received my Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering and Physics from [Sharif University of Technology](https://en.sharif.edu).
I am passionate about exploring various facets of human systems and networks, particularly focusing on social and financial networks. My research interests encompass a wide range of disciplines, including Network Economics, Game Theory, Network Science, and Computational Social Science. I am also deeply intrigued by the process of human decision-making. My research in this area revolves around comprehending and modeling the intricate mechanisms that guide human choices and actions.
